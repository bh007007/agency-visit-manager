2

First-Time Setup (One time only)

The very first time you open the app, a yellow banner will appear at the top asking for the database URL. You only need to do this once per device.

1
You will see a yellow banner: "⚙️ First-time Setup: Paste your Google Apps Script Web App URL..."
2
Ask your manager for the API URL. It looks like:
https://script.google.com/macros/s/XXXXX/exec
3
Paste the URL into the input field and click Connect.
4
The banner disappears and the header shows 🟢 Synced. Setup is complete.
✅
This step is only required once. Your browser remembers the URL automatically. Next time you open the app, it connects right away.
3

Signing In with Your PIN

Every time you open the app, you will see a sign-in screen. Enter the PIN given to you by your manager.

1
Type your PIN in the center box and press Sign In (or press Enter).
2
If the PIN is correct, the dashboard loads immediately.
3
If you see "Incorrect PIN", check that Caps Lock is off and try again.

Two types of accounts:

✏️ Editor
  • ✓ View all visits
  • ✓ Add new visits
  • ✓ Edit existing visits
  • ✓ Delete visits
👁 Viewer
  • ✓ View all visits
  • ✗ Add new visits
  • ✗ Edit existing visits
  • ✗ Delete visits
🔒
Keep your PIN private. Do not share your PIN with others. If you think your PIN has been compromised, contact your manager immediately.

To sign out, click the Sign Out button in the top-right corner of the header. Your session ends automatically when you close the browser tab.

Adding a New Visit Editors only

Click the + New Visit button in the top-right corner. A form will slide open.

Agency / Group Name Name of the agency or group studyhere (PECA group)
Country Code 3-letter country code TWN KOR JPN
Gender / Pax Gender and number of guests M *1 F *2 Mixed *4
Guest Name Full name of the guest or group leader Kelvin
Arrival / Departure Date Select from the date picker Apr 19 – Apr 25

Special Requirements Check all that apply 🚗 Pickup, 🏨 Accommodation, 🎥 Recording

When finished, click Save. The record is saved to the shared Google Sheet instantly — all staff will see it right away.

✅
Auto-sync When you save, the header shows 🟢 Saved. Other staff will see the new visit the next time they refresh the page.
6

Editing or Deleting a Visit Editors only

To edit: Find the visit card and click the ✏️ Edit button. The same form opens with all existing information pre-filled. Make your changes and click Save.

To delete: Click the Delete button on the card. A confirmation popup will appear — click OK to confirm. This cannot be undone.

⚠️
Deletion is permanent. Once deleted, the record is removed from Google Sheets immediately. Always double-check before deleting.

Using the Calendar View

Click the 📅 Calendar tab to switch to the monthly calendar. It shows all visits and scheduled appointments on their dates.

ColorMeaning
BlueGuest is staying on campus (within arrival–departure range)
OrangePickup required on this date
GreenA specific scheduled appointment (visit schedule item)

Click any event on the calendar to open that visit's edit form.

Use the ‹ Prev and Next › buttons to move between months.

Frequently Asked Questions

The app says "Connection failed" — what do I do?
Check your internet connection and refresh the page. If the problem continues, contact your manager to verify the API URL is still active.
I don't see the Edit / Delete buttons on the cards.
You have a Viewer account. Viewer accounts can only read data. If you need to make changes, ask your manager to give you an Editor PIN.
How do I see the latest data added by another staff member?
Simply refresh the page (F5 or pull-to-refresh on mobile). The app loads the latest data from Google Sheets each time you sign in or refresh.
My PIN stopped working.
PINs can be changed by the manager at any time. Contact your manager to get your updated PIN.
Can I use this on my phone?
Yes. Open the URL in your phone's browser (Chrome or Safari). For faster access, save it to your home screen — it will open like a regular app.
Is the data safe?
All data is stored in a private Google Sheet owned by your organization. Access requires a PIN. Never share your PIN or the API URL outside the team.
The yellow setup banner appeared again on a different device.
Each device/browser needs to be set up once. Paste the API URL again and click Connect. This only takes about 10 seconds.

Need help?

Contact your manager or the person who set up this system for assistance.
